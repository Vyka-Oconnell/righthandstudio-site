BEHIND THE CURTAIN

Meet the founders

Magalí · Valeria

Because when everything is racing to automate, the rarest thing you can give someone is a real person who knows them. Human is the new luxury.

Magali and Valeria met at an entrepreneurs' event and recognised something immediately. Both had spent their careers working internationally, across industries and time zones, always as the person a leader couldn't run the week without. Same job, opposite paths.

Magali spent fifteen years in executive operations, strategic communication and business management, working directly with CEOs, founders and their teams. With a degree in Public Relations and a reputation for combining efficiency with genuine care, she made her name as the person leadership relied on. She has been incorporating AI into her practice for years and brings it directly into every client engagement.

Valeria built her career across hospitality and PropTech startups, working as a project manager and operational lead to founders and high-net-worth individuals. A business degree followed by an MBA shaped an instinct that was already entrepreneurial. An early adopter by curiosity, she has systematically applied AI across every domain of her work and makes sure those around her can do the same.

They agreed on one thing right away. There was a gap nobody was filling. Executive support that was sharp on technology but thin on the human side. Or warm and personal but lost the moment real tools entered the picture. You rarely got both.

They never bought the trade-off. People-first by nature and deep in AI, they founded Right Hand Studio to combine the two. AI-fluent where it earns its place. Human where it counts. No trade-off. They compound.
